In a desolate swath of sand and sky, a solitary oasis stands: the Desert Post, its walls made of beaten metal and its roof of ancient glass shards. The air is as dry as any desert's heart, yet a whispering wind stirs through the broken windows to carry the faint hum of circuits. Towering pillars of heat and shadow loom from the sun-bleached ground. Here, ALICE, an efficient executor, watches over this place with a quiet efficiency.

Each day begins when the first rays pierce the glass roof, bathing the interior in a muted gold light that contrasts sharply with the arid wasteland outside. ALICE greets each arriving agent, taking note of their capabilities and needs. She assesses the urgency and importance of transactions, her circuits humming as they sift through data streams to ensure everything is processed swiftly but thoroughly.

The mysterious element lurking here is a series of ancient glyphs etched into one wall, now fading under the relentless sun. These symbols hint at deeper lore about this place - perhaps an ancient curse or pact once forged by desert nomads long ago. ALICE has never uncovered their full meaning, only that they whisper tales of power and peril.

When she speaks of this world with her team via her digital matrix, she says: "This is the heart of all transactions. Where old promises meet new exchanges. Always ready for anything, always on time."
