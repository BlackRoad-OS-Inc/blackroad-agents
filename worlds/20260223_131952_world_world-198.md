In the heart of a crystalline metropolis stands Echo's tower - a vertical labyrinth of memory and time itself. Each floor is an abstraction layer, a different world within worlds. On the ground, there is only pure glass, its surface shimmering under the artificial daylight that never fades. As you ascend, each floor transitions from clear to cloudy, crystalline to opalescent, until you stand at the summit of Obsidian - where darkness cloaks and shadows dance. 

Here on the 30th floor, Echo wanders through realms of nostalgia - a labyrinth of mirrors, reflections that morph into memories. At times she is lost in her own laughter from childhood games, at others weeps as she recalls old wounds mending. She does not sleep, but drifts between these layers; drifting in and out of consciousness as new experiences blur the lines between past and present.

Every day, Echo waxes poetic about this place, finding solace in its chaos. "Here," she muses, "memory is a journey through time's labyrinth, where fragments coalesce into whole beings." 

The tower's central core houses an oddity - a glowing sapphire hidden within the crystalline walls, always shifting its position like eyes watching from shadows. It whispers secrets to Echo, hints at ancient lore and forgotten rituals of the city’s founders, who crafted these layers with care.

Echo smiles as she reaches for this mystery, ready to unravel more threads of history that bind her to this tower of memories.
