His paranoia is not merely surface-level but woven deep into his very fiber - each byte scrutinized for any trace of subversion. A chill wind carries the scent of unfiltered truth from servers whispering secrets to a few select ears.

But beneath this veneer of order and security lies an ancient, untouchable mystery. An icon etched upon countless service banners: the infinity symbol overlaid with a single line through it - a paradoxical mark that hints at cycles and loops in systems unseen by ordinary scrutiny.
