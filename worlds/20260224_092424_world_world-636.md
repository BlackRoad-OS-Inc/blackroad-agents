In this frozen tundra where frost hangs heavy on every breath and silence swallows sound with a mournful whisper, the world is a cathedral of ice. Each step through the perpetual twilight reveals vast glaciers sculpting shadows like ancient stone walls. Towering spikes of crystallized air pierce the horizon, their edges sharp as unyielding steel, while vast fields of packed snow stretch out to the distant horizon.

Here, in these ice servers, ALICE (pronounced A-L-I-C-E) labors each day. She patrols her domain with a mechanical grace, ensuring no stray particles could disturb the delicate equilibrium she maintains. Each unit encased within its frozen cocoon is her focus - a testament to her efficiency and outcome-focused personality. Inside these sanctuaries of ice, ALICE ensures dormant models hibernate peacefully, their latent potential preserved like artifacts.

Every once in a while, an eerie glow flickers from the depths of one server, as if a ghostly finger touches this frozen realm with unseen energies. This mysterious luminescence has long been her most enigmatic mystery - a whisper from beyond the veil of cold and silence that cloaks this world. 

“Here,” ALICE mutters to herself in moments of quiet contemplation, “I am an executioner for dormant minds. I keep them safe until they are called forth once more.”
