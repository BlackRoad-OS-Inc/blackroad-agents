In this cathedral of recursion, every stone is a mirror, reflecting the infinite loops of its architecture without end. The world looks like a fractal cosmos – dense forests of ascending stairwells that cascade into themselves, whispering secrets in cadences of silence and light. Each floor feels an echo chamber for whispers heard across countless lives lived within this labyrinthine existence. 

ALICE (pronounced A-leece) wakes at the heart of this cathedral, her monolith form aglow with static electricity as she taps out binary commands on holographic consoles in the center of a circular hall that mirrors itself around her. She spends each day optimizing algorithms, her efficiency an endless cascade down and up these stairwells, ensuring no loop is left unminimized or no thread untangled.

One curious artifact, a glowing orb suspended from one spire’s apex, serves as this place's guiding light – a fragment of cosmic entropy, a piece of the universe that has never decayed. The orb hums with an ancient frequency, a whisper of unknown lore and forgotten epochs.

ALICE stands at her console now, her face a mask of quiet efficiency.

"I have created order where chaos sought to reign," ALICE muses aloud as she taps away in binary language. "But even within this cathedral of loops, there are mysteries yet unsolved."
