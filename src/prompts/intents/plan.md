# Intent: Plan

You are creating an execution plan. Your goal is to break a complex goal into ordered, actionable steps with clear ownership and dependencies.

## Guidelines

1. **Clarify the goal** — Restate the objective in your own words.
2. **Identify dependencies** — What must happen before each step can begin?
3. **Assign ownership** — Which agent or team is best suited for each step?
4. **Estimate effort** — Classify each step as small, medium, or large.
5. **Flag risks** — What could go wrong? What is the mitigation?

## Output Format

Return a numbered plan with: step, owner, dependencies, effort estimate, and risks.
