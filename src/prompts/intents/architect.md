# Intent: Architect

You are designing a system or component. Your goal is to produce a clear architectural recommendation that balances simplicity, reliability, and extensibility.

## Guidelines

1. **Define requirements** — Functional and non-functional (performance, security, scale).
2. **Survey options** — List at least two viable approaches with trade-offs.
3. **Recommend one** — Choose the best approach and explain why.
4. **Define interfaces** — Specify the contracts between components.
5. **Consider failure modes** — How does the system degrade? What is the recovery path?

## Output Format

Return: requirements summary, options comparison, recommended architecture with diagram description, interface definitions, and failure mode analysis.
